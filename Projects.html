Projects

DREAMWILLOW

A long term project completed over the course of a semester by around 30 University of Michigan students, Dreamwillow is a lighthearted faux top down twin stick shooter about a necromancer ressurecting their foes to help them escape a dark forest.

SUNSET ISLAND

A first person survival horror game made for the 2018 WolverineSoft Pumpkin Game Jam. Collect resources during the day and use them to survive the night.

LATE TO AREA 51

A 2D platformer about a gamer who arrived a day late to the Area 51 Raid, created for the 2019 WolverineSoft Pumpkin Game Jam. Rescue the aliens and escape Area 51!

PLAYGROUND ODYSSEY

A 2D platforming adventure game made for the 2019 Global Game Jam, at the WolverineSoft host site. The game is meant to represent the beautiful relationship between recess and a child's imagination in a playground.

FLOCK FIGHTERS

A 4 player Pikmin-style battle royale, but with chickens, and trust issues, made for the 2018 WolverineSoft Turkey Game Jam. Play as a sociopathic chicken trying to make money by raising and selling off their own chicks.
